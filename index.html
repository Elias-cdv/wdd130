Ecuador

The photo is not the most popular photo about Ecuador. I'm not including the wonderful mountains, or the coast, or the amazonic jungle, nor the galapagos Islands. My city, Guayaquil, is the most populated in the country. Crossed by rivers, the city of Guayaquil is an important port for the country's economy. The Guayas River has a particularity: its flow is mixed, so it goes on both sides. Its climate is humid and hot, so the famous "coconut water" is welcomed by all citizens to hydrate. In this photo, we see how the Chimborazo volcano, despite being more than 100 km from the city, a certain times of the year, when the sun and the clouds are favorable, the city can appreciate the beauty of the imposing volcano.
